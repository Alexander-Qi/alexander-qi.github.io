  13. 给了一大堆 hemoglobin 的突变体， 问各种突变体可能带来什么后果。 这道题和教材上的下面这道题很像（第六版教材 p185）

There are almost 500 naturally occurring variants of hemoglobin. Most are the result of a single amino acid substitution in a globin polypeptide chain. Some variants produce clinical illness, though not all variants have deleterious effects. A brief sample follows.

  • HbS (sickle-cell Hb): substitutes a Val for a Glu on the surface
  • Hb Cowtown: eliminates an ion pair involved in T-state stabilization
  • Hb Memphis: substitutes one uncharged polar residue for another of similar size on the surface
  • Hb Bibba: substitutes a Pro for a Leu involved in an alpha helix
  • Hb Milwaukee: substitutes a Glu for a Val
  • Hb Providence: substitutes an Asn for a Lys that normally projects into the central cavity of the tetramer
  • Hb Philly: substitutes a Phe for a Tyr, disrupting hydrogen bonding at the alpha 1 beta 1 interface

Explain your choices for each of the following:

  1. The Hb variant least likely to cause pathological symptoms.
  2. The variant(s) most likely to show pI values different from that of HbA on an isoelectric focusing gel.
  3. The variant(s) most likely to show a decrease in BPG binding and an increase in the overall affinity of the hemoglobin for oxygen.

    (a) Hb Memphis (b) HbS, Hb Milwaukee, Hb Providence, possibly Hb Cowtown (c) Hb Providence

  1. G、 P、 F、 Y、 W、 K 写出全名； P、 G 对α helix 的影响； Y、 W 侧链的共同特征， 以及这种特征对于测量蛋白质浓度的帮助。

    1. G: glycine, P: Proline, F: Phenylalanine, Y: Tyrosine, W: Tryptophan, K: Lysine
    2. Proline and Glycine make it very difficult to form $\alpha$-helix.
    3. Both Tyrosine and Tryptophan contain a residue that is aromatic, and have a UV absorption, which makes it possible to determine the concentration of a given protein according to the absorption of the solution.

    SH2 is an abbreviation of Src homology 2, so named because the sequence of an SH2 domain is similar to that of a domain in Src (pronounced sark), another protein Tyr kinase. In signal transduction, a P–Tyr residue of IRS-1 binds to the SH2 domain.

简答题

  1. proteoglycan 的定义是什么？ 有什么功能？ 举例， 至少说两个。
    Proteoglycans are proteins that are heavily glycosylated.

  2. cAMP 作用于 PKA 的原理是什么？ 预测什么突变能让 PKA constitutively active？
    cAMP binds to the regulatory subunits of the PKA. And each R subunits binds to two cAMPs and undergoes a dramatic reorganization that pulls its inhibitory sequence away from the catalytic subunit, opening up the substrate-binding cleft and releasing each C subunit in its catalytically active form.

  9. mirror repeat and panlindromic structure 是什么？ 他们通常是什么的识别序列？

    1. The term palindrome is applied to regions of DNA with inverted repeats of base sequence having twofold symmetry over two strands of DNA. When the inverted repeat occurs within each individual strand of the DNA, the sequence is called a mirror repeat.
    2. Many palindromic sequences are the recognition site for DNA restriction enzymes and binding sites for transcription factors.

  12. 怎样用 FRET 测量 cAMP 的浓度。

    The gene for GFP is fused with that for the regulatory subunit (R) of cAMP-dependent protein kinase (PKA), and the gene for BFP is fused with that for the catalytic subunit (C). When these two hybrid proteins are expressed in a cell, BFP (donor; excitation at 380 nm, emission at 460 nm) and GFP (acceptor; excitation at 475 nm, emission at 545 nm) in the inactive PKA (R 2 C 2 tetramer) are close enough to undergo FRET.
